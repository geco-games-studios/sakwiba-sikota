About Sakwiba Sikota

Born on January 3, 1960, Sakwiba Sikota has built a distinguished career in law and public service. A graduate of Keele University in the United Kingdom, he earned an Honors degree in Law and Politics in 1980 before being called to the Zambian Bar in December 1981, having excelled in the Law Practice Institute Bar Examinations with four distinctions.

Our History

Founded in 2010, Horizone began as a small blog run by a group of passionate travelers. What started as a platform to share personal adventures quickly evolved into a comprehensive resource for globetrotters seeking authentic experiences.

Over the years, we've grown from a handful of contributors to a diverse team of travel experts, tech enthusiasts, and creative minds, all united by our love for exploration and discovery.
