About Me

Sakwiba Sikota S.C., born on January 3, 1960, in Luanshya, Zambia, is a seasoned lawyer, politician, and human rights advocate. He holds an International Baccalaureate Diploma (1977) from La Chategnarai, Geneva, an LL.B. (Hons) in Law & Politics (1980) from Keele University, UK, and a Postgraduate Diploma in International Law (1982) from the University of Zambia. Called to the Zambian Bar in 1981, he lectured at the National Institute of Public Administration before working in private law firms and later founding Central Chambers in 1990, where he serves as Principal Partner.
